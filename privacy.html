Privacy Policy

Your secrets are safe in the void.

Last Updated: February 4, 2026

Welcome to Chibi Survivors! We are committed to protecting the privacy of our players ("Survivors"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you play our game.

1. Information We Collect

We believe in minimal data collection. We only collect data necessary to improve your gameplay experience and serve relevant content.

  • Device Information: Model, OS version, and language settings to ensure compatibility.
  • Gameplay Data: Progress, levels unlocked, and in-game economy stats (Gold/Gems) to save your journey.
  • Crash Logs: Anonymous reports to help us fix bugs and glitches.

2. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services to support our game infrastructure (e.g., for analytics, ads, or cloud saves). These partners have their own privacy policies:

  • Unity Ads / Google AdMob: To deliver rewarded video ads.
  • Firebase / Google Analytics: To understand how the game is played and improve balance.
  • Google Play Games / Apple Game Center: For achievements and cloud saves.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To save your game progress and inventory locally or in the cloud.
  • To show optional "Rewarded Ads" (e.g., "Watch Ad for Gems") explicitly requested by you.
  • To analyze specific difficulty spikes (e.g., "Are players dying too much on Map 3?") to balance the game.

4. Children's Privacy

Chibi Survivors is designed for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us immediately, and we will delete it.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your data. You can reset your local save data directly within the game settings ("Delete Account" / "Reset Progress"). For cloud data deletion requests, please contact us.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our game (e.g., new multiplayer features). Significant changes will be announced in the game's news feed.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions, suggestions, or just want to report a bug, reach out to us:
